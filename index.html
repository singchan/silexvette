HOME OF THE WORLD'S 1ST CENTRIFUGALLY SUPERCHARGED C7 CORVETTE, OCTOBER 2014!

A&A Corvette produced the first C7 Corvette supercharger system in October of 2013. After much testing and a few “tweaks” here and there, production systems became available in January of 2014.
